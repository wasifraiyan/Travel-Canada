My favourites

Ripley's Aquarium of Canada

"Ripley's Aquarium, located in the heart of Toronto, offers a captivating journey into the depths of the ocean. Immerse yourself in the beauty and wonder of marine life through interactive exhibits and breathtaking displays."

Bluffers Sand Beach

"Bluffers Sand Beach, located along the shores of Lake Ontario in Toronto, is a hidden gem that offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. With its sandy shores and scenic views, it's a perfect spot for relaxation and outdoor activities."

Toronto Island

"Toronto Island, a serene haven just a short ferry ride from downtown Toronto, offers a tranquil escape surrounded by the beauty of Lake Ontario. Among its hidden gems is the vibrant and lively Harbourfront, a waterfront neighborhood that adds a touch of urban sophistication to the island's natural charm."
